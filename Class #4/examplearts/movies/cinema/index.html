Cinemas

The word "cinema", short for cinematography, is often used to refer to filmmaking and the film industry, and the art form that is the result of it.

Cinema is the form of art that is used to tell a story. It is also a form of communication that connects millions of people through its stories and cinema is the most expensive and collaborative form of art that spends millions of dollars to tell a story.
